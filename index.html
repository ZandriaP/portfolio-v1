About Me

Zandria Puaa is a digital artist based in Hawaii experienced with both traditional watercolor and digital art. She specializes in character design and illustration, and has had experience in album cover, poster, YouTube thumbnail, and game design.

With her work ranging from a cartoon to semi-realistic style, Zandria is inspired by both Western and Eastern animation and video games.

Her passion is to provide a window in which imagination can be seen by the audience, which reflects speculative genres such as fantasy, science fiction, and horror.
